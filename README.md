# SupplySense AI

Predictive inventory and demand forecasting platform for the HORECA industry (Hotels, Restaurants, Cafés).

## Overview

SupplySense AI helps restaurant managers reduce food waste and prevent stockouts by predicting customer demand and recommending supplier orders before stock runs out.

## MVP Features

- **Demand Forecasting** — AI predicts daily covers, peak hours, and menu item demand based on weather, events, and seasonality
- **Inventory Tracking** — Real-time stock levels, expiry dates, and status alerts
- **Ingredient Calculator** — Converts predicted covers into precise ingredient quantities
- **Supplier Orders** — AI-generated purchase order recommendations with manual approval
- **Waste Optimizer** — Detects expiring stock and suggests daily specials to clear it
- **Analytics Dashboard** — Waste trends, forecast accuracy, and supplier spend reports
